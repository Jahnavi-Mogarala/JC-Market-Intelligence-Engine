# JC Market Intelligence Engine - Demo Script

**Step 1: The Hook (Landing Page)**
- Start on the `/` page.
- *Action:* Scroll slowly down the page to show the parallax hero and 3D gradient meshes.
- *Talk Track:* "Welcome to the JC Market Intelligence Engine. The first thing you'll notice is an interface designed not just to be functional, but to be a premium, executive-level experience."

**Step 2: The Initiation (Input Page)**
- *Action:* Click the "Start Intelligence Scan" CTA button.
- *Action:* On the input page, type `Stripe` for Company and `Fintech Payment Infrastructure` for Category.
- *Action:* Hit the glowing "Initiate Autonomous Scan" button.
- *Talk Track:* "We just give the engine a target and a category. The rest is autonomous."

**Step 3: The Dashboard (Live AI Research)**
- *Action:* Let the UI route to `/dashboard`. Watch the nodes light up.
- *Action:* Hover over the active nodes to show tooltips.
- *Talk Track:* "Under the hood, we are spinning up four AI agents. You can see them working in real-time. The Research Agent is gathering unstructured data, the Competitor Agent is building a threat matrix, and the Outreach Agent is crafting personalized copy based on the findings."

**Step 4: The Results (Final Report Page)**
- *Action:* The page automatically transitions to `/report` when the progress hits 100%.
- *Action:* Point to the "JC Intelligence Score" and "Market Threat Index".
- *Talk Track:* "In under a minute, we have a comprehensive, boardroom-ready report. Notice our proprietary Intelligence Score and Market Threat Index. This isn't just raw data; it's synthesized strategy."

**Step 5: The Action (Outreach Console)**
- *Action:* Scroll to the bottom or click the "Outreach Console" tab.
- *Action:* Toggle the "Tone" switch between "Aggressive" and "Consultative".
- *Action:* Copy the drafted email.
- *Talk Track:* "And because intelligence without action is useless, our system drafts hyper-targeted, ready-to-send emails to key decision-makers. You literally click copy, and you're ready to close."

**End of Demo**
